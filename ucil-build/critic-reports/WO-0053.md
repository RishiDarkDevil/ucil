### Soft notes / warnings

1. **`PROTOC` build dependency.**  Several transitive deps under
   `lancedb 0.16` (notably `lance-encoding-build` and `lance-file`)
   require `protoc` on the build host.  A fresh shell without
   `apt-get install protobuf-compiler` or `PROTOC=…` exported will
   fail AC09 / AC11 / AC12 / AC13 / AC15 at the
   `lance-encoding-build.rs` step.  Forward-looking — the host on
   which the verifier already passed clearly has it.  No source
   defect.

2. **One commit (`faacb88`) is dominated by `Cargo.lock` churn.**
   ~4609 lines of lockfile delta.  The 32-line `Cargo.toml` and
   9-line `crates/ucil-daemon/Cargo.toml` deltas are well under the
   soft target.  Lockfile updates cannot atomically split from the
   workspace-dep addition.  Acceptable.

3. **Commit 2 (`f51c34b`) is 352 lines.**  Skeleton commit covers
   module-level rustdoc preamble (~70 lines), `BranchManagerError`
   enum with 6 doc-commented variants (~70 lines), `BranchTableInfo`
   + `BranchManager` struct + 4 accessor methods + the private
   `sanitise_branch_name` helper, and the lib.rs `pub mod` insertion.
   Falls under `DEC-0005` module-coherence carve-out (≤ ~200 LOC was
   the historic ceiling; 352 is wider but the constituent sub-units
   are tightly coupled — splitting `BranchManagerError` from
   `BranchManager` produces dead-code intermediate state because the
   error type is referenced in every method's `Result` return).
   Acceptable but at the edge — consider a planner micro-ADR to
   formally bump DEC-0005's ceiling for skeleton commits if the
   pattern recurs.

4. **AC17 regex remains a footgun.**  Even though the retry-2 fix
   wrapped the 6 doctest assertions in `let _ = …`, a future PR that
   adds `///[space]assert!(…)` anywhere in `branch_manager.rs` would
   resurrect the failure.  The frozen acceptance shape comes from
   the work-order; out-of-critic-scope to amend.  Forward-looking
   note for the planner — flag for a tightened regex
   (`^[[:space:]]*//[^/]`) in a follow-up ADR.

5. **`tempfile.workspace = true` listed twice in
   `crates/ucil-daemon/Cargo.toml`.**  Lines 77
   (`[dependencies]`) and 80 (`[dev-dependencies]`).  Pre-existing
   in HEAD; not introduced by this WO (visible in the diff because
   the new `lancedb` / `arrow-*` lines land alphabetically adjacent
   under `[dependencies]`).  Out-of-scope soft note.

6. **Branch recreation pattern.**  Per the retry-2 ready-for-review
   marker (`ucil-build/work-orders/0053-ready-for-review.md:66-77`),
   the executor used `git push origin --delete` (a delete-ref, not a
   force-push) plus local `git branch -D` plus a re-create from

   compliant with the anti-laziness contract: no `--amend` after
   push, no force-push.  The retry-1 sha chain (`e8b5a88…7b0932a`)
   is preserved in `git reflog` and in the prior rejection / RCA
   reports.  No action required.
