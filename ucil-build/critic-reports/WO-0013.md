1. **`.expect()` outside `#[cfg(test)]`** — `crates/ucil-cli/src/commands/plugin.rs:292`:

   ```rust
   1 => Ok(matches
       .into_iter()
       .next()
       .map(|(_, m)| m)
       .expect("len() == 1 above guarantees one element is available")),
   ```

   `.claude/rules/rust-style.md` §Errors states _"Never `.unwrap()` / `.expect()` outside `#[cfg(test)]`.
