## Scope

WO-0043 extends `crates/ucil-daemon/src/plugin_manager.rs` with two
master-plan ┬¦14.2 capabilities:

1. **Hot-reload** (P2-W6-F03): `PluginManager::reload(name)` drains
   in-flight tool-call readers via the runtime's `tokio::sync::RwLock<()>`
   gate, then runs a real `health_check` to swap the plugin without
   restarting the daemon. On success the runtime flips to `Active`,
   `last_call` advances, and `restart_attempts` resets to 0.
2. **Circuit breaker** (P2-W6-F04): `PluginManager::restart_with_backoff(name)`
   loops `MAX_RESTARTS = 3` times with exponential backoff
   (`base ├Ś {1, 2, 4}` where `base` defaults to 1 s); after exhaustion
   the runtime is marked `Error` and `PluginError::CircuitBreakerOpen`
   surfaces. The default base is configurable per-test via
   `PluginManager::with_circuit_breaker_base`, letting the acceptance
   test compress the production 7 s wall to ~35 ms.

Two new module-root acceptance tests land at the frozen feature-list
selectors (`plugin_manager::test_hot_reload`,
`plugin_manager::test_circuit_breaker`).
