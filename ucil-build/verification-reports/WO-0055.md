    index_repo, load_index_to_sqlite, query_symbol, ScipError, ScipG1Source, ScipReference,

`ScipError` is re-exported from the `scip` module via `pub use` — is met.
A single-line form would conflict with `cargo fmt --check` (also an
acceptance criterion at line 129) which would re-wrap a long brace block.
Treating this as a substantive PASS: substance met, format-only mismatch.
